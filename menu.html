About us

If you're looking for wings and brews you've come to the right place. We take our sauces serious and our sodas in glass bottles. We're ecstatic to bring fresh food and a chill spot to the neighborhood. We ain't kidding when we say Rooster's is a fun joint to hang your hat and receive a free beer. We bring you everything but the kitchen sink since 2016.
